Age Suitability

Age suitability information for MoofDash.

Calculated Age Rating

MoofDash is intended as a utility companion app for VanMoof S3 riders. Based on its current content and features, MoofDash does not include mature, user-generated, social, messaging, advertising, medical, or unrestricted web-browsing content.

Content Summary

  • No profanity or crude humor.
  • No horror or fear themes.
  • No alcohol, tobacco, drug use, or references.
  • No medical or treatment information.
  • No user-generated content, social feed, or in-app chat.
  • No advertising.
  • No unrestricted web access.

Intended Use

MoofDash is designed for users who own or operate a supported bike and want companion controls, ride-related information, NFC-supported actions, Bluetooth connectivity, maps, and media controls.
