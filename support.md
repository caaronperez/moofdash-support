# MoofDash Support

Support for MoofDash, an iOS companion app for VanMoof S3 riders.

## Contact

For app support, bug reports, or privacy questions, email [criistianaaron@gmail.com](mailto:criistianaaron@gmail.com).

## Bluetooth Connection

Make sure Bluetooth is enabled, the bike is nearby, and MoofDash has Bluetooth permission in iOS Settings.

## NFC Card Scanning

Use a physical iPhone with NFC support. Hold only one NFC card near the top of the phone while scanning.

## Location and Maps

If ride or map features do not work as expected, confirm that MoofDash has location permission in iOS Settings.

## Media Controls

Media and Spotify features may require Apple Music or Spotify permissions and an active connection to the selected service.
