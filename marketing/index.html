MoofDash

A focused iOS companion app for VanMoof S3 riders.

Ride Controls

MoofDash brings common bike controls, ride information, and companion features into a clean iPhone experience.

Bike Companion Features

  • Connect to supported VanMoof S3 bikes with Bluetooth.
  • Use NFC-supported features when available on your device.
  • View map and ride-related information when location permission is granted.
  • Use media controls while riding when connected media services are available.
