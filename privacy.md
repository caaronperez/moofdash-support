# MoofDash Privacy Policy

Last updated July 14, 2026.

MoofDash is an iOS companion app for VanMoof S3 riders. This policy explains what information the app uses to provide its features.

## Information MoofDash Uses

- **Bluetooth:** Used to discover and connect to your bike.
- **NFC:** Used when you choose to scan a bike card or NFC tag.
- **Location:** Used for map, route, or ride-related features when you grant permission.
- **Media access:** Used for playback controls and music-related features when you grant permission.
- **Spotify authorization:** Used only if you connect Spotify. Tokens may be stored securely on your device so the app can keep the connection working.

## Data Storage

MoofDash does not operate its own account system. App preferences, bike settings, NFC identifiers, and connected-service tokens may be stored locally on your device or in secure iOS storage such as Keychain where appropriate.

## Third-Party Services

MoofDash may use Apple system services, Spotify, and Google Maps features. Those services may process data according to their own privacy policies when their features are used.

## Data Sharing

MoofDash does not sell personal information. The app does not intentionally share personal information with third parties except as needed to provide features you choose to use, such as Spotify playback or map functionality.

## Diagnostics

Apple may provide crash logs or diagnostics to developers when users opt in through iOS settings. These diagnostics are used to improve app stability.

## Your Choices

You can change Bluetooth, Location, Media, and other permissions in the iOS Settings app. You can also disconnect connected services such as Spotify by removing the app's authorization from that service.
