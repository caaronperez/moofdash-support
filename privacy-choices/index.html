Privacy Choices

Control the permissions and connected services used by MoofDash.

iOS Permissions

You can manage MoofDash permissions in the iOS Settings app. Open Settings, choose MoofDash, then review permissions such as Bluetooth, Location, Media, and NFC-related access.

Spotify Connection

If you connect Spotify, you can disconnect MoofDash by removing the app's authorization from your Spotify account settings.

Local App Data

MoofDash stores app preferences and connected-service information locally on your device or in secure iOS storage where appropriate. You can remove local app data by deleting MoofDash from your device.
